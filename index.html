Who am I?

I was born 1999, The last possible year to join an elite class, consisting of 20th century people.

Smögen is a place where I spend a lot of time. I have both friends and family over there. The boys and I try to gather there every winter, easter and summer. You could call it our headquarters.

Healthy Eating

Eating well is something I've started both thinking & reading about a lot. When I was younger I blindly bought products where the ingredients-list looked like scientific study litterature, without ever questioning what I actually was putting into my body. Now I follow a few simple rules when consuming food and beverages:

  1. An ingredient should NOT contain multiple ingredients.
  2. Seed oils should be avoided if possible. (Since it is often used in non-nutritional foods)
  3. If possible, choose grassfed meat & eggs.
  4. Tactically consume carbs when you need it as fuel.
  5. Today might be the last day. If the rules can't be followed, don't feel guilt, instead, ENJOY YOURSELF!
